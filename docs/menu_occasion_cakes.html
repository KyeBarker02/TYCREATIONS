Decoration

Birthday cakes are designed around your theme, colour palette, and the personality of the person being celebrated. No two are ever the same.

Number & Letter Cakes
Sculpted number or letter-shaped cakes — filled with cream and fruit, and topped with beautiful fresh florals.
Themed & Character Designs
From favourite colours to hobbies, passions, and themes — designed around the person, not a template.
Sprinkles & Confetti

From simple posies to elaborate floral crowns — in any colour to match your theme or season.
