Presentation

Every bakery order is presented with the same care and attention as our cakes — whether it's a personal treat or a thoughtful gift.

Chocolate Drizzle
Brownies finished with a glossy Belgian chocolate drizzle — dark, milk, or white — for an indulgent final touch.
Luxury Gift Boxes
Beautifully presented in luxury boxes with tissue paper and ribbon — ideal for birthdays, occasions, or corporate gifting.
Edible Toppings
Brownies can be finished with additional toppings — crushed nuts, caramel drizzle, sea salt flakes, or chocolate shavings.
Personalised Tags
Custom handwritten or printed gift tags added to your order — perfect for weddings, events, or special occasions.
Ribbon & Wrapping
Ribbon-tied boxes or individually cellophane-wrapped pieces — a beautiful finish for events and wedding favours.
Pie Presentation
Pies are presented in their baking dish and boxed ready to serve — rustic, warm, and utterly homemade in feel.

* More complex or bespoke decoration choices may affect the final price. Full details will be discussed and confirmed at the enquiry stage.
