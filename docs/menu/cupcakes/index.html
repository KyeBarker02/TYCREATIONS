Decoration

Small bakes can be finished to match a theme, colour palette, or wedding stationery. Every detail is considered.

Piped Buttercream Swirls
Classic rosettes, ruffles, or flat palettes in any colour — a beautiful and versatile finish for cupcakes.
Royal Iced Shortbread
Flood-iced shortbread in any design — from monograms to florals, wedding motifs, or custom illustrations.
Edible Glitter & Lustre
Dusted edible shimmer — gold, silver, pearl, or coloured — for a magical, sparkling effect.
Dried Flower Toppers
Tiny dried rose buds, lavender sprigs, or chamomile pressed into buttercream for a botanical touch.
Gift Packaging
Individual clear boxes, ribbon-tied favour bags, or luxury gift boxes with personalised tags.
Personalised Toppers
Names, initials, or dates piped in chocolate or crafted as edible wafer paper toppers.

* More complex or bespoke decoration choices may affect the final price. Full details will be discussed and confirmed at the enquiry stage.
