About Me

The Baker
Behind the Cakes

My name is Tetiana and where I come from, Ukraine, desserts are an important and meaningful tradition. Growing up learning to bake through lessons passed down between generations, it became a beloved hobby. When life brought my daughter and I to the UK a few years ago, I began a new chapter, building a life filled with meaning and care for my daughter. Here, I also found the love of my life. Today I am happily married, surrounded by a family that inspires everything I create.

It was here that I was able to transform baking, having started as a personal hobby, into a business built with love. Today, I create handcrafted cakes, carefully balanced in flavour, for the people of Eastbourne and the surrounding areas.

Each cake is crafted with precision and designed to feel truly special. To me, they are not just desserts, they are a feeling, a moment, a memory.

Always made by hand and always made with love.

Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

How far in advance should I order? +
I recommend placing your order at least 2 weeks in advance to secure your preferred date. Last-minute orders may occasionally be available depend my schedule.
Can I fully customise my cake? +
Absolutely! Each cake is designed to suit your celebration. You can choose the flavour, size, colours, decorations, and overall style.
How can I pay? +
A 50% deposit is required to secure your booking. The remaining balance is due before collection or delivery. Payments can be made by bank transfer or cash.
Is the deposit refundable? +
The deposit is non-refundable, as it secures your date and covers preparation costs. If you need to change your date, I will do my best to accommodate your request, subject to availability.
What flavours do you offer? +
I offer a wide range of flavours, including:
  • Signature Honey Cake (Medovik)
  • Classic Vanilla
  • Rich Chocolate
  • Lemon & Elderflower
  • Funfetti
  • Red Velvet
  • Carrot & Spice
  • Carrot & Caramel
  • Black Forest
  • Snickers
  • Banana Cake
  • Pavlova
  • Fraisier
  • Nutella
  • Pistachio & Raspberry
  • Raffaello
  • Oreo
  • Mango & Raspberry
  • Tropical
  • Brownie
  • Kinder Bueno
Seasonal flavours and custom combinations are also available — if you have something special in mind, I'd love to create it for you.
How do I choose the right size? +
The ideal size depends on the number of guests.
  • 6" cake – serves 10–12
  • 8" cake – serves 20–24
  • 10" cake – serves 35–40
  • 12" cake – serves 55–60
If you're unsure, I'll be happy to help you choose.
Do you offer delivery? +
Yes. Delivery is available for an additional charge, calculated in our enquiry form.
Can I send inspiration photos? +
Of course! Inspiration photos are always welcome. They help me understand your vision, although every cake is uniquely created and may not be an exact replica.
Do you cater for allergens and dietary requirements? +
Please let me know about any allergies or dietary requirements when placing your order. While I take every precaution, all cakes are prepared in a kitchen that handles common allergens, including dairy, eggs, nuts, gluten, and soy.
What information do you need when I place an order? +
To create the perfect cake, I’ll need your event date, number of servings, preferred flavour, design ideas, and any allergy information. Inspiration photos are always welcome.
