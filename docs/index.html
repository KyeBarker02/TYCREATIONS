About Me

The Baker
Behind the Cakes

My name is Tetiana and where I come from, Ukraine, desserts are an important and meaningful tradition. Growing up learning to bake through lessons passed down between generations, it became a beloved hobby. When life brought my daughter and I to the UK a few years ago, I began a new chapter, building a life filled with meaning and care for my daughter. Here, I also found the love of my life. Today I am happily married, surrounded by a family that inspires everything I create.

It was here that I was able to transform baking, having started as a personal hobby, into a business built with love. Today, I create handcrafted cakes, carefully balanced in flavour, for the people of Eastbourne and the surrounding areas.

Each cake is crafted with precision and designed to feel truly special. To me, they are not just desserts, they are a feeling, a moment, a memory.

Always made by hand and always made with love.

The Honey Cake

This is the cake I'm most known for — a refined version of the traditional Medovik. Soft, delicate honey layers with a light, fragrant cream that feels both comforting and quietly elegant.

Available in four sizes and a range of beautiful flavour combinations, the Honey Cake is my signature — and exclusive to TY Creations in Eastbourne.
