01

Who We Are

TY Creations Ltd is a registered limited company in England and Wales. We are the data controller responsible for the personal information collected through this website.

For the purposes of UK data protection law, including the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018, TY Creations Ltd is the controller of your personal data.

TY Creations Ltd
Registered in England and Wales
Contact: tycreations.eastbourne@gmail.com

If you have any questions about how we handle your personal data, please contact us at the email address above before submitting a formal request.

02

What Data We Collect

We only collect information that you voluntarily provide to us through the enquiry form on this website. This may include:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your email address
  • Your event date
  • The type of cake or bake you are enquiring about
  • The number of guests or servings required
  • Your delivery postcode (used only to calculate a delivery estimate)
  • Any additional details you choose to share in the free-text message field, which may include dietary requirements or design preferences

We do not collect any special category data (such as health information) unless you choose to share it voluntarily in your message, for example to inform us of a food allergy.

We do not collect payment information through this website. Any payment processing is handled separately and directly between us.

03

How We Use Your Data

We use the information you provide solely to respond to your enquiry and, where relevant, to discuss and fulfil a cake order with you. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Respond to your enquiry by email
  • Discuss your order requirements, including size, flavour, design, and delivery
  • Calculate and confirm a delivery cost based on your postcode
  • Contact you about the status of your order if one is placed

We will not use your data for marketing purposes, and we will not send you promotional emails unless you have separately and explicitly opted in to receive them.

We will never sell your data to any third party.

04

Legal Basis for Processing

Under UK GDPR, we are required to have a lawful basis for processing your personal data. We rely on the following:

  • Legitimate interests — when you contact us with an enquiry, we have a legitimate interest in processing your data in order to respond to you. This processing is necessary and proportionate, and we do not believe it overrides your rights and interests.
  • Contractual necessity — where an order is placed, processing your name, email, and delivery details is necessary to fulfil that contract.
  • Legal obligation — we may be required to retain certain records for tax and accounting purposes in our capacity as a limited company.
05

Third Parties & Data Processors

We use a small number of third-party services to operate this website. These services may process your personal data on our behalf as data processors. We have ensured each is appropriate for use under UK GDPR:

  • Formspree — our enquiry form is processed by Formspree, Inc. Formspree stores form submissions securely and forwards them to our email address. Formspree operates in accordance with GDPR and offers an EU data region, which we have selected. Their privacy policy is available at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.
  • hCaptcha — our enquiry form is protected by hCaptcha, a privacy-friendly CAPTCHA service operated by Intuition Machines, Inc. hCaptcha processes behavioural data to distinguish human users from automated bots. It does not use data for advertising purposes. Their privacy policy is available at hcaptcha.com/privacy.
  • Google Fonts — this website loads fonts from Google Fonts. When your browser requests a font, your IP address is sent to Google's servers. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • GitHub Pages — this website is hosted by GitHub Pages (Microsoft). GitHub may log standard server access data such as IP addresses. Their privacy statement is available at docs.github.com.

We do not share your personal data with any other third parties unless we are legally required to do so.

06

Cookies

This website does not use any tracking, analytics, or advertising cookies. The only cookies that may be set are those introduced by third-party services listed above (specifically Google Fonts and hCaptcha), which are technically necessary for those services to function.

We do not use cookies to track you across other websites, and we do not use any advertising or profiling cookies.

07

How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain personal data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Enquiries that do not result in an order — we will retain your data for up to 12 months in case you re-enquire, after which it will be securely deleted.
  • Completed orders — we retain order-related correspondence and basic customer details for up to 6 years in line with our obligations as a UK limited company under the Companies Act 2006 and HMRC requirements.

If you would like us to delete your data sooner, please contact us and we will do so promptly, provided we are not legally required to retain it.

08

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights in relation to your personal data:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — you can ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure — you can ask us to delete your personal data, subject to any legal retention obligations.
  • Right to restrict processing — you can ask us to limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability — you can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object — you can object to us processing your data on the basis of legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at tycreations.eastbourne@gmail.com. We will respond within one calendar month of receiving your request, in line with our obligations under UK GDPR.

We will not charge a fee for handling your request unless it is manifestly unfounded or excessive.
