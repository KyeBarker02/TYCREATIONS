Decoration

Wedding cakes are designed around your theme, colour palette, and the personality of the person being celebrated. No two are ever the same.

Number & Letter Cakes
Sculpted number or letter-shaped cakes — filled with cream and fruit, and topped with beautiful fresh florals.
Themed & Character Designs
From favourite colours to hobbies, passions, and themes — designed around the person, not a template.
Sprinkles & Confetti
Edible confetti, star sprinkles, and rainbow jimmies — celebratory, fun, and full of joy.
Fresh & Sugar Flowers
From simple posies to elaborate floral crowns — in any colour to match your theme or season.
Drip & Ganache Finishes
A luxurious drip of white, dark, or coloured chocolate ganache down the sides of the cake.
Ombre & Gradient
Smooth colour gradients blended by hand — from soft pastels to bold saturated tones.
Age & Name Toppers
Elegant hand-piped names or ages, or bespoke acrylic and sugar toppers to commemorate the occasion.
Smash Cake
A small matching smash cake for baby's first birthday — a sweet, messy tradition we love.

* More complex or bespoke decoration choices may affect the final price. Full details will be discussed and confirmed at the enquiry stage.
