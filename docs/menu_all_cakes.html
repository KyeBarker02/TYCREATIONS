Banana Cake

Nutella

Pistachio Raspberry

Raffaello

Oreo

Decoration

From effortlessly simple to breathtakingly ornate — every finish is discussed and designed together with you.

Smooth Buttercream
A clean, palette-knife-finished coat in any colour — classic and elegant in its simplicity.
Sugar Flowers
Hand-sculpted sugar roses, peonies, anemones, and botanicals — indistinguishable from the real thing.
Hand-Painted Designs
Watercolour-effect painting, marbling, or illustrated motifs applied directly to the cake surface.
Gold & Metallic Finishes
Edible gold leaf, lustre dust, or full metallic tiers for a glamorous, high-impact finish.
Fondant & Sculpted Finishes
Smooth or textured fondant, ideal for intricate bas-relief patterns and sharp geometric shapes.
Fresh & Dried Botanicals
Seasonal flowers, greenery, and dried botanicals — always food-safe and beautifully arranged.
Lambeth Piping
For the classic, vintage look. Done in buttercream to add an elegant touch.
Personalisation
Names, monograms, dates, and messages written in edible ink or sculpted in sugar.
