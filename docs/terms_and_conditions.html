01

Placing an Order

To ensure we can serve you to the highest standard, please place your order at least 10 days in advance of your event date. All orders are prepared individually and scheduled ahead of time, so advance notice is essential.

Last-minute orders may be considered at our discretion, subject to availability. We cannot guarantee the ability to fulfil orders placed with less than 10 days' notice.

An order is not confirmed until a deposit has been received. Submitting an enquiry or receiving a quote does not constitute a confirmed booking.

02

Order Confirmation & Deposit

Orders are confirmed only upon receipt of a 50% non-refundable deposit of the total agreed order value. Your order date will not be held or reserved until this deposit has been received.

Once your deposit is received and your order is confirmed, you will receive written confirmation of your order details, including the agreed cake description, event date, collection or delivery arrangement, and outstanding balance.

Please review your order confirmation carefully and notify us of any errors within 48 hours of receipt.

03

Payment

We accept payment by bank transfer or cash. All prices are quoted and payable in pounds sterling (GBP).

  • Deposit (50%) — due at the time of booking to confirm your order
  • Remaining balance (50%) — due upon collection or prior to delivery

We reserve the right to withhold collection or delivery of your order until the outstanding balance has been received in full.

All prices quoted are final for the agreed specification. Any changes to the order after confirmation may result in a revised quote, which must be agreed in writing before the change is applied.

04

Order Changes

We understand that plans can change. Where possible, we will accommodate reasonable amendments to your order, subject to the following:

  • All changes must be requested in writing (by email) and confirmed by us before they take effect
  • Requests to change flavour, size, design, or quantity must be made at least 7 days before your event date
  • Changes requested with less than 7 days' notice cannot be guaranteed and may not be possible
  • Changes that increase the order value will require payment of the additional amount before the change is confirmed
  • Changes that reduce the order value will not result in a refund of the deposit
05

Cancellation Policy

If you need to cancel your order for any reason, please notify us in writing as soon as possible.

The 50% deposit is non-refundable in all circumstances. This reflects the time, planning, and ingredients already committed to your order at the point of booking.

  • Cancellation more than 7 days before the event — deposit is forfeited; no further charge applies
  • Cancellation within 7 days of the event — deposit is forfeited; the full remaining balance may be invoiced where ingredients have already been purchased or preparation has begun

In the unlikely event that we need to cancel your order due to circumstances beyond our control (such as illness or a serious emergency), we will notify you as soon as possible and refund your deposit in full. This is the limit of our liability in such circumstances.

08

Care of Your Cake

To ensure your cake arrives and is served at its best, please follow the care guidance we provide with your order. In general:

  • Most cakes should be stored in a cool, dry place and kept out of direct sunlight and heat
  • Cakes containing fresh cream, fruit, or perishable fillings must be refrigerated and consumed within the timeframe stated on your order confirmation
  • Allow refrigerated cakes to come to room temperature for at least one hour before serving for the best flavour and texture
  • Tiered cakes should be transported flat and on a stable, level surface — never on a car seat

We will always provide specific care instructions with your order. We cannot accept responsibility for deterioration caused by failure to follow these guidelines.
