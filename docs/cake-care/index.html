Your Guide

Care Instructions

Thank you for choosing TY Creations. To enjoy your cake at its very best, please follow these simple care guidelines.

Storage

Keeping Your Cake Fresh

  • Keep refrigerated until serving
  • Store in its box or an airtight container to prevent absorbing fridge odours
  • Best enjoyed within 3–4 days of collection or delivery
Before Serving

Getting the Best Flavour

  • Remove from the refrigerator 1–2 hours before serving
  • Allow to come to room temperature for the best flavour and texture
Transportation

Transporting Safely

  • Always transport on a flat surface
  • Keep the vehicle cool, especially during warm weather
  • Avoid placing in direct sunlight
Freezing

Freezing for Later

  • Most sponge cakes can be frozen for up to 1 month
  • Wrap individual slices well before freezing
  • Defrost overnight in the refrigerator before enjoying
Decorations

What to Remove Before Serving

  • Remove fresh flowers, dried flowers, cake toppers, candles, and all other non-edible decorations before serving
  • Sugar paste decorations are edible but may be firm in texture
Allergen Information

Allergens

Our cakes may contain or come into contact with:

Gluten Milk Eggs Nuts Peanuts Soy

Please inform us of any allergies before ordering. We are happy to discuss your requirements and explore options where possible.
