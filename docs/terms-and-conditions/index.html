Care of Your Cake

Specific care and storage instructions will be provided with your order. Please follow them carefully. General guidance applies as follows:

  • Most cakes should be kept in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and heat sources
  • Cakes containing fresh cream, fresh fruit, or other perishable fillings must be refrigerated and consumed within the timeframe stated in your care instructions
  • Allow refrigerated cakes to reach room temperature for at least one hour before serving for best flavour and texture
  • Tiered or structured cakes must be transported on a flat, stable, level surface — never on an inclined car seat or held unsecured during transit
  • Cakes should not be consumed beyond the use-by guidance provided

We cannot accept responsibility for deterioration in quality, appearance, or safety caused by failure to follow the care instructions provided with your order.
