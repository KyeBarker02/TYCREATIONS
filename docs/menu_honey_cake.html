Flavours

The Honey Cake is built on our signature golden honey sponge. Choose from our signature flavour combinations below, or customise with your favourite fruit filling.

Signature Flavours

Classic Honey Cake
Delicate honey sponge layers with silky vanilla cream cheese frosting on whipped cream — soft, balanced, and full of rich honey flavour
Blueberry & Lemon Curd
Soft honey sponge layered with vibrant blueberry compote, silky lemon curd and vanilla cream cheese frosting — a fresh and perfectly balanced combination
Caramel Honey Cake
Caramelised honey sponge layers with smooth caramel cream and delicate notes of salted caramel — rich, soft, and deeply comforting
Chocolate Honey Cake
Chocolate honey sponge layered with silky chocolate cream — rich yet delicate, combining deep cocoa flavour with soft caramelised honey layers
Orange Curd
Light honey sponge paired with vibrant orange curd and vanilla cream cheese frosting — fresh citrus notes perfectly complement the rich honey layers
Raspberry Curd
Traditional honey sponge layered with bright raspberry curd and smooth vanilla cream cheese frosting — a beautiful balance of sweetness and berry freshness

03

Decoration

Every Honey Cake includes a signature honey drizzle finish. Additional decoration is bespoke and discussed at the enquiry stage.

Signature Honey Drizzle
Included with every cake — a glistening golden honey drizzle cascades down the sides for a naturally beautiful finish.
Dried Florals
Pressed dried flowers and botanicals — lavender, chamomile, or rose petals — arranged delicately on top.
Gold Leaf Accents
Edible gold leaf applied by hand for a touch of luxury — beautifully paired with the honey tones.
Fresh Herbs & Fruits
Seasonal fresh herbs like rosemary or thyme, or fresh fruits such as figs, raspberries, and lemon slices.
Honeycomb Shards
Real honeycomb or sugar-spun honeycomb shards placed on top for texture, drama, and sweetness.
Hand-Painted Inscriptions
A name, date, or short message painted by hand in edible gold or natural food colouring.
