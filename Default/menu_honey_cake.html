Flavours

The Honey Cake is built on our signature golden honey sponge. Choose your filling and frosting to make it your own.

Sponge

Golden Honey
Our signature — a light, fragrant honey sponge with warm floral notes
Honey & Lavender
Honey sponge infused with a delicate Provençal lavender
Honey & Lemon
Bright citrus zest woven through the honey sponge
Honey & Almond
A nutty warmth paired with the sweetness of wildflower honey

Filling & Frosting

Honey Buttercream
Silky, classic and richly sweet — the perfect complement
Lemon Curd & Cream
Tangy lemon curd layered with whipped cream cheese frosting
Chamomile & Vanilla
Floral chamomile whipped with smooth vanilla bean paste
Mascarpone & Fig
Seasonal fig preserve with light whipped mascarpone
Salted Caramel
House-made salted caramel swirled through Swiss meringue buttercream
Clotted Cream & Raspberry
Fresh raspberries with a Devon clotted cream frosting
03

Decoration

Every Honey Cake includes a signature honey drizzle finish. Additional decoration is bespoke and discussed at the enquiry stage.

🍯
Signature Honey Drizzle
Included with every cake — a glistening golden honey drizzle cascades down the sides for a naturally beautiful finish.
🌸
Dried Florals
Pressed dried flowers and botanicals — lavender, chamomile, or rose petals — arranged delicately on top.
✨
Gold Leaf Accents
Edible gold leaf applied by hand for a touch of luxury — beautifully paired with the honey tones.
🌿
Fresh Herbs & Fruits
Seasonal fresh herbs like rosemary or thyme, or fresh fruits such as figs, raspberries, and lemon slices.
🐝
Honeycomb Shards
Real honeycomb or sugar-spun honeycomb shards placed on top for texture, drama, and sweetness.
🖋️
Hand-Painted Inscriptions
A name, date, or short message painted by hand in edible gold or natural food colouring.
