About Me

The Baker
Behind the Cakes

Hi, I'm TY — the baker, designer, and one-woman team behind every TY Creations cake. I started baking from my home kitchen out of a simple love for bringing joy to people through food, and what began as weekend projects for friends and family has grown into something I feel truly proud of.

Every cake I make is crafted by hand from start to finish — from baking and filling to sculpting decorations and adding the finishing touches. I use only the finest ingredients and pour real care and attention into every single detail, no matter the size of the order.

My approach is always personal. I take the time to understand your vision, your occasion, and the person being celebrated — so that the cake I create feels uniquely yours. I believe that a truly special cake should be as beautiful to look at as it is delicious to eat.
